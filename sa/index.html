What Is Syringe Assist?

Syringe Assist is an organizer which maintains sterility of needles and cannulas for the nurses and doctors who perform cosmetic injections. It is made of clear acrylic plastic, which is light, sturdy, elegant, and easily wiped clean.

Syringe Assist can safely hold:

  • Syringes from size BD to 5cc
  • Needles from 18g up to 25g
  • Cannulas of all sizes
  • A variety of up to 3 syringes at a time

Dimensions:

  • Base is 6" x 6"
  • Height is 6.5"
